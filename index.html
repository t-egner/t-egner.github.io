SILVESTER FINNIGAN

Trained in solo triangle at the Conservatory of Berlin, Silvester spent most of his youth dancing to techno from 12:01 AM until 3:34 AM. Silvester Finnigan, now a real-estate tycoon, spends most of his days writing Dada poetry and living as a nihilist. He also owns a zoo, where is feeds the goldfish to the lions and keeps a couple tarantulas on the loose to give the visitors a good scare. His left big toe is very numb.
