CHRISTIAN SARREL - Professionally affiliated with Pinkin Lark since lunch today. Sarrel is a connoisseur of all things that make noise rhythmically, though he specializes in playing the triangle in an eclectic nu-metal style. Aside from the noise he contributes to Lark's sonic profile, not much has ever been heard from Sarrel's voice. Despite this, perhaps due to telepathy, his backing vocals come through loud and clear through the mic. In addition, legend has it his forehead has not seen the light of day in over ten years on account of his mega-edgy hair.
